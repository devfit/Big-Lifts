Wendler-5-3-1

Follow Jim Wendler's 5/3/1 lifting program to increase strength and lifting ability. Get the app, and get lifting.

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Features.

  • Breakdown Wendler lift numbers by week and lift
  • Add and modify lifts
  • Log and track your lifts through the 5/3/1 cycles

Something missing?
